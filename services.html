Face Recognition based Attendance System



A Face Recognition based Attendance System for Classroom Environment proposed an attendance system that solves the manual method of existing system. It is face recognition method to make the attendance. The system even facial expression lighting and pose of the person for making attendance. For managing student and lecture attendance, a system has been put in place. Time and effort are reduced, especially when there are many students and attendance is being tracked for lectures. Python is the programming language used to implement the entire system. The system makes use of facial recognition technology to track student attendance. Additionally, this student record may be used for exam-related matters. On this project, there are still some tasks to complete in order to notify the student about his or her attendance by SMS. For this, a GSM module is employed. This SMS alert is sent to the student's parent.
